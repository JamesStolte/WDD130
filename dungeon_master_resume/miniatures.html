Miniatures and Painting

Miniatures are a pillar of tabletop role playing games, and yours should look good while you play. We can help your miniatures look like the powerful characters that you envision! Here are some examples of our previous commissions. We can paint them for you, or we can help you to make your own masterpiece. Send us an email detailing your miniature and preferred color scheme, and watch us work our magic to help you work yours!
