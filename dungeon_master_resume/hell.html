A 'Hellish' Party

A Harvest to Remember

Character Level: 15

Playtime (Hours): 3-4

Recommended number of players: 4-5

Difficulty: Hard

A friend in a high place has invited you to a harvest party in his mansion. Unfortunately, the Hells power waxes strong this time of year, and the Demon Lords are bored. One spices up the evening by having some fun with the partygoers. The players are thrust into an arena in one of the nine hells and fight for their lives against abominations and demons alike.
