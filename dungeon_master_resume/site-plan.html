Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to show my skills and experience in running games of Dungeons and Dragons, and showcasing the campaigns and custom characters that I create. I intend to advertise my services to those who would want to play D&D.

Audience

I am advertised to Dungeons and Dragons players of all ages as well as people who are looking for someone to lead their games or give them inspiration for a short quest line for their own game. I am also for people who like painting miniatures and are interested in seeing my work.

Content

Home page

Introduction Custom Adventures is here to send you on your next exciting adventure! Want to battle the Kraken onboard a stolen pirate ship? Want to befriend a lost pixie and delve into the feywild? Or do you simply want help painting your next miniature masterpiece? We at Custom Adventures have many prepared quests and adventures for your tabletop RPG playgroup. We offer complete sets of miniatures and maps to go along with each adventure, or feel free to customize the stories to your own campaign! We are here to give you inspiration on your next character and will even paint them for you! Check out our prewritten one-shot adventures ranging from levels 5-15! Being a Dungeon Master is easy with Custom Adventures!

Campaigns

Campaigns Running from a ghost pirate ship, hunting for a long lost city on a newly discovered and dangerous continent, going to a formal ball with a 'demonic' twist, and hunting down the remnants of a dead god's cult. These adventures and more can be found here. For beginners, we recommend the 'Lost City of Orazca' questline. It is fairly straightforward and helps players and DM's alike to better understand the mechanics of Dungeons and Dragons and how your playgroup works together. Feel free to expound and change things in your campaign according to your will! For experienced players, we recommend the 'Cult of the Deep One' storyline. It is a lower level campaign, but we feel that adds to the challenge of the quest. Players must solve the mystery of the terrible smell coming from the sewers and find the island where an ancient, arcane ritual is about to commence! Regardless of your experience and play style, we have a campaign for you! Have any ideas for a possible campaign or one-shot? Message us with the details and we can write one for you. Dungeon Masters for Hire A common problem with a group of people that wants to play Dungeons and Dragons is that everyone wants to be a player, and no-one wants to run the game. Luckily, we solve that problem for you. We can provide you with a dungeon master to run any of our campaigns or one-shots and allow you forever DM’s to finally play that character you’ve had waiting in the wings for three years. Our skilled DM’s will provide you with a fun and satisfying weekend. If you find one you like, you can request them to run a longer campaign for your group (up to the specific DM’s discretion). Meet the DM Hello! My name is James and I am a Dungeon Master, chief writer for all of our campaigns and one-shots, and the founder of Custom Adventures. I have been a Dungeon Master for three years and have run many adventures and one-shots of all varieties. I have commandeered all of the adventures that we publish and love them all to bits! Feel free to contact me with any questions about my availability and campaign ideas. My favorite adventure to run is the ‘Cult of the Deep One,’ one of my first written adventures and one that I ran for my personal play group. The Lost City of Orazca On a recently discovered continent, there are rumors about a city with ancient, powerful artifacts and riches beyond your wildest dreams. Thought to be lost, your pirate commander sends you out with a traitor as a guide to find this fabled city in an unforgiving jungle… The Cult of the Deep One The sewers have a rancid smell coming from them, what could be the cause? Search the sewers and find traces of an ancient god, and the profane ritual that is about to commence… A ‘Hellish’ Party Your friend invites you to a harvest festival party that he is hosting. Unfortunately, a bored demon lord decides to make the night… interesting. Feyry Deep Danger When an injured Faerie Dragon falls in the party’s lap and asks for help, there is no doubt that the feywild is in grave danger…
