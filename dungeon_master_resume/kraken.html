The Cult of the Deep One

The Summoning

Character Level: 5-7

Playtime (Hours): 3-4

Recommended number of players: 4-6
(or any even number)

Difficulty: Moderate

Your party is called by the owner of a bar that you frequent in a coastal city to investigate the terrible smell coming from the sewers. It is driving away his customers and he is willing to pay you to get rid of it. Down there you find traces of combat and dead creatures you don't know the name of, but they reek! How does the black robed figure in the shadows relate to the smell, and what is up with the map they dropped?
