Feyry Deep Danger

The Dragon Lillith

Character Level: 10-12

Playtime (Hours): 2-4

Recommended number of players: 4

Difficulty: Medium

While the party is walking through a dense section of forest, a small fairy draon by the name of Lilly falls from the sky, bleeding from wounds. After recovering, she leads the party to the feywild to help. The feywild, usually bright and mysterious, is cloaked in a dark mist, becoming sinister and dangerous. Can the party help fight this darkness?
